About me

My name is Rolandas Eitutis and I am a student in KEA. I began my Multimedia studies there in 2017 autumn. I have a background in art studies, which came from getting my art school diploma in 2013 back in my home country Lithuania. Design and multimedia have always been one of the things I took interest in, but nearing my graduation from high school I decided to take the path to becoming an actual Multimedia Designer. In person I would describe myself as a "friendly if spoken to" kind of guy, since my hobbies usually don't align much with other people, so it's hard to strike a conversation first, but otherwise I like talking to people and making new friends. Traveling is also one of the things I do commonly, which is probably what every other international student would tell you ;)
